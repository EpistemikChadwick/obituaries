Jack Elton Crowe
August 21, 1927 - June 4, 2015

Jack Elton Crowe went to be with the Lord on June 4, 2015.  Jack was born on August 21, 1927 in Webster City, Iowa.  He served in the U.S. Army at West Point, New York and while stationed there he met the love of his life, Mary Ellen Curry.  They were married on May 16, 1948.  He was drafted by the Brooklyn Dodgers and played semi-pro baseball in Texas.  Jack attended TCU and retired from TXI as an electrical engineer.  He enjoyed reading, playing golf and attending the local “Spit and Whittle Club”.  Jack is survived by his wife, Mary, children, Pat Crowe and wife, Reta, Steve Crowe and wife, Leisha and Laurie Austin and husband, Terry, along with 6 grandchildren, and 8 great-grandchildren.  A special thank-you to the wonderful staff at Baylor, Scott & White Waxahachie for their care.  In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the Midlothian Bible Church.  Memorial services will be held at 11:00 a.m. on Saturday, June 6, 2015 at Midlothian Bible Church.
